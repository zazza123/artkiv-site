Built by an artist, for artists

Artkiv was born from a personal need to organize a growing art collection and track sales without complicated spreadsheets or expensive software.

Why Artkiv?

As a creative, I struggled with managing my art collection and tracking sales across multiple platforms. Existing tools were either too complex, too expensive, or required constant internet. Artkiv solves all these problems with a simple, beautiful, and completely offline solution.

Our Promise

We believe your creative data should be yours alone. That's why Artkiv is offline-first and privacy-focused. No accounts required, no data collection, no subscriptions needed for essential features. Just download and start organizing your art.
