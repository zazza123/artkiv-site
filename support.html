Help & Support

Find answers to common questions and learn how to get the most out of Artkiv.

Getting Started

Welcome to Artkiv! Here's everything you need to know to get started with managing your artwork collection.

What is Artkiv?

Artkiv is an offline-first mobile application designed specifically for illustrators, artists, and creatives. It helps you:

  • Archive your artwork - Store photos, descriptions, and metadata for all your pieces
  • Track sales - Record transactions, buyers, and earnings
  • Manage expenses - Keep track of supplies, commissions, and business costs
  • Analyze performance - View charts and reports about your creative business

Key Features

  • 100% Offline - All data is stored locally on your device. No internet required.
  • Privacy-Focused - Your data never leaves your device. We don't collect any personal information.
  • Secure - Protect your archive with biometric authentication or passcode.
  • Beautiful Interface - Modern, intuitive design that makes organizing art enjoyable.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Is Artkiv really free?

Yes! Artkiv is free to download and use. The free version includes all essential features for managing your artwork collection. We offer a Premium subscription that removes ads, transactions and backup daily limits.

Does Artkiv require an internet connection?

No! Artkiv is designed to work completely offline. All your data is stored locally on your device. You only need an internet connection if you choose to view ads (free version) or manage your Premium subscription.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! Your data never leaves your device. We don't have servers that store your artwork, transactions, or any personal information. You can also protect access to Artkiv with biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint) or a passcode.

Which devices are supported?

Artkiv is available for both iOS and Android (Coming Soon) devices. It's optimized for phones but also works great on tablets.

Data & Backup

How do I backup my data?

Since Artkiv stores data locally, your data is included in your device's standard backup:

  • iOS: Enable iCloud Backup in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > iCloud Backup
  • Android: Use Google Backup in Settings > System > Backup

We strongly recommend keeping regular backups to prevent data loss.

Can I transfer data to a new device?

Yes! When you restore your new device from a backup, your Artkiv data will be transferred automatically. For iOS, use iCloud or iTunes backup. For Android, use Google Backup or transfer tools provided by your device manufacturer.

What happens if I uninstall the app?

Important: Uninstalling Artkiv will delete all data stored by the app on your device. Make sure you have a device backup before uninstalling if you want to preserve your data.

Premium & Subscription

What does Premium include?

Premium subscription includes:

  • Ad-free experience
  • Unlimited artworks, transactions, and sales records
  • Unlimited backups
  • Priority access to new features
How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Artkiv
  • Android: Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > Artkiv

Canceling will stop future charges, but you'll keep Premium access until the end of your billing period.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple or Google, not directly by us. To request a refund:

  • iOS: Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Android: Visit Google Play Help Center

Troubleshooting

The app is running slowly. What can I do?

Try these steps to improve performance:

  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart the app
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest version of Artkiv installed
  • Ensure your device has sufficient storage space
Images are not loading properly. How can I fix this?

If images aren't displaying correctly:

  • Check that you've granted Artkiv permission to access your photos
  • Restart the app
  • Make sure your device has enough storage space
  • Try reimporting the affected images
I forgot my passcode. How do I recover access?

If you've set up biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint), try using that to access the app. Unfortunately, if you've forgotten your passcode and cannot use biometrics, the only option may be to reinstall the app, which will delete your data. This is why we recommend keeping regular device backups.
